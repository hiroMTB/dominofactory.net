bio

Hiroshi Matoba

Freelance artist/programmer, born in Japan in 1983. He studied product design and acoustic design in Kyushu University and started making handmade sequence system for unknown rhythm research. In 2007 he made software sequencer "Overbug" which implements circular notation system and was invited to transmediale and another media art festival in Italy, Argentina, Taiwan, etc. He also made installation with hardware device such as “light valve(installation for diy transparent display)” or “denki domino(light and sound domino)”. Currently based in Berlin.
